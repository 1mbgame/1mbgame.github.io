Bunny Rescue

1. This is run and jump funny game. It added bubble effect of the bunny when you consume the blue magic bottle.

2. Each Level have few missions need to be completed. The higher level, the more difficult of the missions.

3. Each level-5,10,15,20 are running mode. Where the bunny will auto running and you have to tab the screen to jump over stumble rocks. It will have monster chasing you at the high level game.

4. Collect more diamonds, you will receive 25 bonus of gold coins for each diamond.

5. Once you activated the full version, you can buy or sell your item in the shop with gold coins. You also can buy the magic-bottle from the shop.
