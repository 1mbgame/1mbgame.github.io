Duck War

The game is very easy to play, simply tap to shoot. If you like to play hunting game, then this is the fun game for you. Play this game on your android phone or tablet, this is perfect game for making tea break and suitable for children and adult for all age people who like to play casual game.

Lets go for the duck hunting today.

Game feature:
– Added New and popular social system – Swarm. Which allow you to share your high score with friends from facebook, google, and twitter.
– Compete the score from worldwide shooter, simple and easy to add them in your friend list.
– Support high/low definition graphics and fully optimized the game play.
– The duck moving speed will slowly getting faster with the level increase, try your best and beat your own record and your friends.

New Game Features:
– Added new Bosses system. Total up to 10 differences bosses with special abilities on each boss.
– The boss will drop the powerful plasma bomb to the land and deduct your score, hit the plasma bomb before it hit on the ground, the game now is more challenging than before.
– Added new Achievement Systems. And total up to 50 achievements metal allow you to collect it.
– Now you can enjoy hitting your highest score. Challenge you fast respond reaction, and improve your finger fastest speed.
